European Family Trip Ideas from PDX

Reykjavik, Iceland

Travel: Non-stop PDX -> KEF | Flight Time: ~7 hr 35 min

Top Day Trips & Walks

  • The Golden Circle: Easy, flat walking paths right between tectonic plates and geysers.
  • Reykjadalur: A moderate valley hike ending in a naturally warm river you can bathe in.
  • City Center: Walk around Tjörnin (city pond) and the Harpa Concert Hall interactive play areas.

Amsterdam, Netherlands

Travel: Non-stop PDX -> AMS | Flight Time: ~9 hr 35 min

Top Day Trips & Walks

  • Zandvoort aan Zee: 30-min train to the coast for wide beaches and easy dune walks.
  • Zaanse Schans: Short trip north for historic windmills and live demonstrations.
  • Vondelpark & Jordaan: Massive car-free park walking and picturesque canal streets.

Frankfurt, Germany

Travel: Non-stop PDX -> FRA | Flight Time: ~10 hr 20 min

Top Day Trips & Walks

  • The Rhine Valley: Short train to riverboats, dotted with hillside castle ruins.
  • Taunus Nature Park: Gentle, heavily wooded trails ending at a reconstructed Roman fort.
  • Main River Promenade: Flat, tree-lined walking paths starting right from the old town square.
